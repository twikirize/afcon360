## When you SHOULD split (not now)

You split **only when one of these happens**:

1. File becomes **hard to reason about**
2. Merge conflicts increase
3. Different devs touch different identity areas
4. You add **more identity subdomains** (KYB, Trust, Risk, AML, etc.)

You’re *approaching* that point — but not forced yet.

## What should NEVER be in identity

This is important ⛔

Do **not** put these in identity:

* Orders
* Trips
* Listings
* Products
* Payments logic (wallet is fine, transactions are not)
* Reviews

Those modules should **reference identity by ID only**.
