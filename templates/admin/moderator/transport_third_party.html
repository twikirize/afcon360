Role Guidelines
Moderator Role
Can review applications for basic compliance, flag problematic requests, and provide initial recommendations.
  • Review basic compliance requirements
  • Check documentation completeness
  • Flag suspicious applications
  • Provide initial recommendations
Admin+ Role
Can approve technical requirements, manage API access, and handle business terms negotiations.
  • Review technical specifications
  • Approve API access credentials
  • Negotiate business terms
  • Make final approval decisions
Owner Role
Can approve strategic partnerships, authorize revenue sharing, and oversee major integration decisions.
  • Approve strategic partnerships
  • Authorize revenue sharing
  • Review legal frameworks
  • Approve deep platform integration
