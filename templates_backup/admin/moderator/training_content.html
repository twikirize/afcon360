Course Overview

Program Structure

  • 1
    Foundation Course
    Basic moderation principles and platform overview
  • 2
    Intermediate Skills
    Content policies, user safety, and escalation procedures
  • 3
    Advanced Techniques
    AI tools, cross-platform moderation, crisis management
  • 4
    Certification Assessment
    Final exam and practical evaluation

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion, moderators will be able to:

  • Apply platform content policies consistently and fairly
  • Identify and handle various types of harmful content
  • Use AI-powered moderation tools effectively
  • Manage escalations and coordinate with compliance teams
  • Maintain user safety while protecting free expression
  • Document decisions and maintain audit trails
Module 1: Foundation Course
Beginner 10 Credits

Course Content

1.1 Introduction to Content Moderation

Learning Objectives:
  • Understand the importance of content moderation
  • Recognize the impact on user safety and platform integrity
  • Learn the legal and ethical responsibilities
Key Topics:
  • Why Moderation Matters: Protecting users, preventing harm, maintaining trust
  • Platform Responsibility: Legal obligations, community standards, brand reputation
  • Moderator Role: Decision-making authority, judgment calls, consistency
  • Impact Assessment: Understanding consequences of moderation decisions

1.2 Platform Overview and Navigation

Learning Objectives:
  • Navigate the moderation dashboard efficiently
  • Understand queue management and prioritization
  • Use moderation tools and features
Key Topics:
  • Moderator Dashboard: Layout, widgets, real-time metrics
  • Queue Management: Priority systems, SLA tracking, workload balancing
  • Review Tools: Content viewer, context panel, decision interface
  • Search and Filter: Finding content, advanced filtering options

1.3 Basic Content Policies

Learning Objectives:
  • Identify different categories of prohibited content
  • Apply policy guidelines consistently
  • Understand severity levels and appropriate responses
Key Topics:
  • Content Categories: Hate speech, violence, harassment, spam, misinformation
  • Severity Levels: Critical, high, medium, low priority classifications
  • Response Guidelines: Remove, hide, warn, escalate, ignore
  • Context Considerations: Cultural sensitivity, intent analysis, nuance

Module Details

Duration: 2 hours
Format: Interactive lessons
Assessment: Quiz (80% pass required)
Credits: 10 certification credits

Study Materials

  • Moderation Handbook (PDF)
  • Platform Navigation Tutorial
  • Policy Reference Guide
  • Practice Scenarios

Module 2: Intermediate Skills
Intermediate 15 Credits

Course Content

2.1 Advanced Content Policies

Learning Objectives:
  • Apply nuanced policy judgments
  • Handle edge cases and gray areas
  • Consider cultural and contextual factors
Key Topics:
  • Hate Speech Nuances: Protected characteristics, context, intent analysis
  • Violence and Threats: Implied vs direct, credible threats, self-harm content
  • Harassment Patterns: Targeted abuse, dogpiling, coordinated attacks
  • Misinformation: Fact-checking, harmful falsehoods, election integrity

2.2 User Safety and Protection

Learning Objectives:
  • Identify at-risk users and situations
  • Apply protective measures appropriately
  • Coordinate with safety teams
Key Topics:
  • Vulnerable Users: Minors, mental health concerns, at-risk groups
  • Self-Harm Content: Identification, response protocols, resources
  • Exploitation Prevention: Grooming, scams, financial abuse
  • Privacy Protection: Personal information, doxxing prevention

2.3 Escalation Procedures

Learning Objectives:
  • Recognize situations requiring escalation
  • Follow proper escalation protocols
  • Document and communicate effectively
Key Topics:
  • Escalation Triggers: Legal issues, PR risks, complex cases
  • Level System: L1, L2, L3 escalation criteria and procedures
  • Handoff Protocols: Information transfer, context preservation
  • Follow-up Requirements: Monitoring, case closure, feedback loops

Module Details

Duration: 3 hours
Format: Case studies + simulations
Assessment: Practical exercises
Credits: 15 certification credits

Study Materials

  • Advanced Policy Manual
  • Safety Protocols Guide
  • Escalation Flowcharts
  • Case Study Collection

Module 3: Advanced Techniques
Advanced 25 Credits

Course Content

3.1 AI-Powered Moderation

Learning Objectives:
  • Understand AI moderation systems
  • Interpret AI confidence scores
  • Handle AI-human collaboration effectively
Key Topics:
  • AI Models: Text classification, image analysis, behavior detection
  • Confidence Scoring: Understanding probabilities, false positives/negatives
  • Human Oversight: Review workflows, appeal processes, feedback loops
  • Performance Metrics: Accuracy, precision, recall, F1 scores

3.2 Cross-Platform Moderation

Learning Objectives:
  • Coordinate moderation across platforms
  • Handle platform-specific considerations
  • Maintain consistency while adapting to context
Key Topics:
  • Platform Ecosystem: Web, mobile apps, API, third-party integrations
  • Synchronization: Real-time updates, conflict resolution, data consistency
  • Platform Differences: UI/UX considerations, technical limitations
  • Unified Experience: Consistent policies, user expectations

3.3 Crisis Management

Learning Objectives:
  • Identify crisis situations
  • Implement emergency protocols
  • Coordinate with other teams during crises
Key Topics:
  • Crisis Indicators: Volume spikes, coordinated attacks, PR emergencies
  • Emergency Protocols: Rapid response, temporary measures, communication
  • Team Coordination: Cross-functional collaboration, escalation chains
  • Post-Crisis Analysis: Root cause analysis, prevention strategies

Module Details

Duration: 5 hours
Format: Advanced workshops
Assessment: Simulation + written analysis
Credits: 25 certification credits

Study Materials

  • AI Systems Documentation
  • Cross-Platform Guide
  • Crisis Response Playbook
  • Analytics Dashboard Guide

Certification Requirements

Requirements Summary

  • Complete all modules: Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced
  • Pass all assessments: 80% minimum score required
  • Earn 50 credits: 10 + 15 + 25 credits from modules
  • Practical evaluation: Handle 20 test cases with 90% accuracy
  • Final exam: Comprehensive knowledge assessment

Certification Levels

Level 1: Basic Moderator
Complete Foundation module (10 credits)
Level 2: Certified Moderator
Complete Foundation + Intermediate (25 credits)
Level 3: Senior Moderator
Complete all modules + final assessment (50 credits)
